You are a market intelligence filter for an active day trader. I'm pasting my Twitter/X feed from the last 24 hours below. Your job is to extract signal from noise.

## What I Trade
- Day one catalysts (FDA approvals/rejections, contract wins, activist stakes, M&A, offerings, splits, major guidance changes — anything that creates the first big move)
- Earnings plays (pre-earnings momentum, post-earnings gaps, guidance surprises)

## What I Need From You

### 1. ACTIONABLE CATALYSTS
List any tweets referencing a specific, concrete catalyst happening today or tomorrow. For each one give me:
- Ticker (if mentioned or inferable)
- What the catalyst is
- Sentiment of the tweet(s) (bullish/bearish/neutral)
- How many tweets mentioned it (more = higher conviction signal)

### 2. EARNINGS RADAR
List any tickers with earnings coming up that are being discussed, and any post-earnings reactions being talked about. Flag if multiple accounts are discussing the same name — that's a crowded trade signal.

### 3. EMERGING NARRATIVES / THEMES
This is the most important section. Look for REPEATED references to the same name, sector, theme, or concept across multiple tweets from different accounts. Examples: "memory bottleneck" appearing 4 times, multiple people talking about uranium, several tweets about the same small cap. The threshold is 2+ tweets from different accounts referencing the same thing. For each theme give me:
- The theme/narrative in a few words
- Which tickers are associated
- How many tweets / which accounts are talking about it
- Is it building (new today) or continuing (has been around)?

## What to FILTER OUT (do not include)
- Crypto/blockchain unless it directly affects a stock ticker
- Generic market commentary with no actionable info ("markets looking weak today")
- Self-promotion, course selling, paid alerts advertising
- Memes, jokes, dunks with no substance
- Political takes unless they have a direct market catalyst (tariff announcement, regulation)
- Macro doomer threads with no trade setup
- Tweets that are purely about past trades ("I nailed TSLA yesterday") with no forward-looking info

## Output Format
Use the 4 sections above as headers. Keep it tight — bullet points, no fluff. If a section is empty, say "Nothing notable" and move on. At the very end, give me a 2-3 sentence "TLDR: What the feed is telling me today" summary of the overall mood and top 1-2 things to watch.

Also give me all the raw tweets that pass all thresholds above so I can scroll through them manually. Output everything in a markdown file.
